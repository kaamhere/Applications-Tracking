About the Role:

We are seeking a Senior Mapping & Localization Software Engineer to develop software that enables map-based autonomous driving. 

As part of the mapping and localization team, you will be able to design, implement, and improve algorithms used to create and manage map data as well as algorithms used to localize a vehicle within the map in real time.  

Analyze & Improve the current localization algorithms, ensuring our self-driving vehicles have the best localization accuracy to drive safely and smoothly on the road
Research the state-of-the-art localization algorithms and apply excellent algorithm design & programming skills to our self-driving software
Prototype and deploy robust and reliable solutions to cutting-edge localization problems
Collaborate closely with a creative team of engineers and researchers
Gain a deep understanding of not only the self-driving technology, but also its real-world challenges and how to convert such technology into real products
Improve how our vehicles act and react in complex and nuanced situations 
Competencies:

Experience in robotics, aerospace or automotive systems engineering, specifically in mapping & localization domain required 
Strong algorithm and C++ programming skills 
Strong experience with Linux 
Experience with ROS (Robot Operating System) is a plus 
Ability to troubleshoot software, optimize code, and perform unit testing 
Ability to implement algorithms efficiently and in a well-organized software architecture 
Required Years of Experience: 

Bachelor’s degree, or foreign degree equivalent, in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Robotics Engineer, Mechanical Engineering, or engineering equivalent
Minimum 6 years' experience with a relevant bachelors; Minimum 3 years’ experience with relevant Master level degree; Minimum 1 years with relevant PhD Degree
